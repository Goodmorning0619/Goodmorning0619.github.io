シャルル

Introduction

シャルル (Charles) is a song created by バルーン(Barun)in his second album “Corridor” released in 2016. He used V Flower to generate the vocal and also released a version of himself covering it.

It is a song about although the character paid a lot of effort into maintaining the relationship with his lover but still ends up breaking up.

On April 24, 2023, a voice drama based on the story of the song was released and featured by 伊東健人 and 原紗友里.
