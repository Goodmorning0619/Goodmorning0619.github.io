https://strike.amorphic.space/

Week 2

1. What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?
The huge "blank" black part that we can draw.
2. Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.
First I tried to draw something in the blank spaces, and I tried to change the brushes. Then I tried to change the color but found out there was only black and white.
3. What part of the experience did you spend the most time engaging with?
Drawing with different brushes.
4. What was the most common action in your two minute interaction with the experience?
Draw random things in the blank spaces.
5. What is your impression of the intended primary goal of the interactive experience?
It is free for everyone to draw anything they want in the blank space.
6. What is your impression of how the interactive experience communicates its primary goal?
They made the webpage looks like common computer-aided design software so that visitors will get the idea immediately when seeing the webpage.
7. What is your impression of how the experience should be interacted with over time? (For how long and how many different times)
I think this will not attract visitors to keep visiting the website because there are a lot of apps that are much easier to approach and use.
8. What is your impression of how the interactive experience communicates how it should be interacted with over time?
Adding more functions to it and maybe adding a bit task-based tutorial.

Week 3

9. What other media forms (digital or otherwise) does the experience reference?
I found the function of this website is familiar to Paint, but black and white version, and have fewer functions. And like Clip Studio Paint, Photoshop, SAI, etc.
10. What does this reference or references suggest to you about how you should act when engaging with it?
The blank part just reminds me of the canvas, and the bar at the left and top reminds me of the tool we usually see in CAD software.
11. What does this reference or references suggest to you about how you should feel when engaging with it?
Feel free to draw anything and be curious about all of the functions they provide so that I can use the software or website more efficiently.
12. What is the most frustrating element of the interaction to you and what makes it frustrating?
Creating a new canvas or deleting the things I have drawn on it. There is not a button for me to delete the layer or even create a new layer.
13. What is the most satisfying element of the interaction to you and what makes it satisfying?
I like it to get rid of all of the colors so that I do not have to think about what color should I use and what color will match it, which kind of simplifies the way using it.
